About

Connecting statistics with meaningful health research

I am a professor and consultant in biostatistics and medical research with extensive experience in study design, statistical analysis, postgraduate supervision, research writing, and professional training.

My work focuses on helping clinicians, public-health professionals, postgraduate researchers, and academic institutions conduct scientifically rigorous, transparent, and publication-ready research.

I also explore practical and ethical applications of artificial intelligence in research education, scientific writing, data analysis, and evidence synthesis.
