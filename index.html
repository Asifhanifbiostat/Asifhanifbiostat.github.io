About

Connecting statistical science with meaningful health research

I am a professor and consultant in biostatistics and medical research with extensive experience in study design, statistical analysis, postgraduate supervision, scientific writing, and professional research training.

My work focuses on helping clinicians, public-health professionals, postgraduate researchers, and academic institutions conduct scientifically rigorous, transparent, reproducible, and publication-ready research.

I have contributed to multidisciplinary research across medicine, public health, dentistry, rehabilitation, epidemiology, allied health sciences, and medical education.

I also explore practical and ethical applications of artificial intelligence in research education, scientific writing, statistical analysis, literature synthesis, and academic productivity.

Areas of expertise

Research support from concept to publication

Biostatistics

Research Methodology

Research questions, objectives, study designs, protocols, sampling strategies, measurement, and methodological review.

Clinical Research

Randomized controlled trials, diagnostic studies, intervention studies, clinical outcomes research, and protocol development.

Epidemiology and Public Health

Cross-sectional, case-control, cohort, surveillance, population-based, and health-outcome studies.

Systematic Reviews and Meta-analysis

Evidence synthesis, meta-analysis, meta-regression, risk-of-bias assessment, critical appraisal, and PRISMA-compliant reporting.

Regression and Statistical Modelling

Linear, logistic, ordinal, count, survival, repeated-measures, multilevel, and multivariable statistical models.

Scale Development and Validation

Questionnaire development, reliability, exploratory factor analysis, confirmatory factor analysis, psychometrics, and item analysis.

Diagnostic Accuracy and ROC Analysis

Sensitivity, specificity, predictive values, likelihood ratios, ROC curves, area under the curve, and optimal cut-off estimation.

Scientific Writing and Publication

Manuscript development, reporting-guideline compliance, reviewer responses, journal selection, publication strategy, and editorial support.

Research Software Training

Applied training in SPSS, R, JASP, AMOS, statistical analysis, interpretation, and publication-ready presentation of results.

Postgraduate Research Supervision

Research mentoring, thesis planning, methodology review, statistical guidance, manuscript development, and publication support.

Artificial Intelligence for Researchers

Responsible use of AI for literature work, research writing, analysis support, teaching, evidence synthesis, and workflow improvement.
