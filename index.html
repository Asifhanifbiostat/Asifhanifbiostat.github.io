Academic profile

About me

Connecting Biostatistics with meaningful health research

I am a professor and consultant in biostatistics and medical research with extensive experience in study design, statistical analysis, postgraduate supervision, scientific writing, and professional research training.

My work focuses on helping clinicians, public-health professionals, postgraduate researchers, and academic institutions conduct scientifically rigorous, transparent, reproducible, and publication-ready research.

I have contributed to multidisciplinary scholarship across medicine, public health, dentistry, rehabilitation, epidemiology, allied health sciences, and medical education.

A growing part of my academic work examines practical and ethical applications of artificial intelligence in research education, scientific writing, statistical analysis, and evidence synthesis.
